The Story

A timeless sanctuary
by the sea.

In Mikuriya, Daisen Town, Tottori Prefecture.
A single house stands quietly in this port town.
This is a private villa, exclusive to one group per day.
Open the door, and the sea breeze and the sound of waves will wash away the hustle and bustle of daily life.

A trip where family and friends gather, a gentle time with loved ones.
In this spacious house that can accommodate up to 14 people, you don't have to worry about anyone else, even if the children's laughter echoes.
Two staircases, maze-like rooms, and the sea filling the windows.
Don't just "stay", but "live" in this town with your precious people.

The Room

Spacious comfort for up to 14 guests

Living & Play

The open living room overlooking the sea is the center where everyone gathers. Heat up with a game of ping pong or play some music. A space filled with smiles where you won't get bored even on rainy days.

Kitchen & Patio

A full kitchen where you can freely cook with fresh local ingredients from Daisen. The courtyard is equipped with a BBQ grill. Spend irreplaceable time cooking and eating together.

Ocean Bath

Bath time bathed in the sunset. Spacious enough to enjoy with children.

Bedrooms

Multiple bedrooms to ensure privacy. Safe layout even for large groups.

Curated Experiences

Experiences

Boat Fishing at Mikuriya Port

A special option in collaboration with local fishing boats. The view of Mt. Daisen from the sea is breathtaking. Enjoy BBQ with the fish you caught at night.

02

Dinner at "Mikuri"

If you prefer not to cook, we recommend a special dinner at the nearby partnered facility "Mikuri". Enjoy dishes using seasonal ingredients from Daisen.

03

Play Inside

Ping pong, board games, Electone. We have prepared entertainment so you won't get bored even on rainy days. Children can also spend time without getting tired.
